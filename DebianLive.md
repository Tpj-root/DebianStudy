**light → heavy table** of Debian 13 Live ISOs with approximate **RAM needs**:

| ISO Variant       | Size     | Desktop Type       | RAM Need (min–recommended) | Notes                             |
| ----------------- | -------- | ------------------ | -------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| **standard**      | \~1.8 GB | No GUI (text only) | 256 MB – 512 MB            | Fastest, pure terminal            |
| **lxde**          | \~3.5 GB | Lightweight        | 512 MB – 1 GB              | Very light, old hardware friendly |
| **xfce**          | \~3.5 GB | Lightweight        | 512 MB – 1 GB              | Light + modern, best balance      |
| **lxqt**          | \~3.7 GB | Lightweight-Modern | 768 MB – 1.5 GB            | A bit heavier than LXDE/XFCE      |
| **mate**          | \~3.6 GB | Medium-light       | 1 GB – 2 GB                | Classic GNOME2 style              |
| **cinnamon**      | \~3.8 GB | Medium-heavy       | 2 GB – 4 GB                | Polished, heavier effects         |
| **gnome**         | \~3.8 GB | Heavy              | 2 GB – 4 GB                | Default Debian desktop            |
| **kde**           | \~3.8 GB | Heavy              | 2 GB – 4 GB                | Feature-rich, very customizable   |
| **debian-junior** | \~4.1 GB | Custom/educational | 1 GB – 2 GB                | For kids/learning, extra packages |

---

* **Best choice**: **XFCE** (smooth, light, modern).
* **If you like fancy look**: Cinnamon.
* **If you want ultra-light**: LXDE.
